Rooms & Facilities

A Cozy Room With Essential Amenities For A Comfortable Stay.

Why Our Rooms So Special?

Our beach hotel offers a variety of rooms designed to suit every guest's needs. Whether you choose the cozy Standard Room, the spacious Deluxe Room, or the family-friendly Family Room, you'll enjoy a restful stay with modern amenities and beautiful views.

For those seeking extra luxury, our Suite Room provides a lavish retreat with a separate living area and premium features. Along with our exceptional rooms, we offer a range of world-class facilities to make your stay unforgettable, including a vibrant bar, rejuvenating spa, refreshing pool, and a fully equipped conference hall for business events. Whether you're here for relaxation or work, our hotel promises a memorable and comfortable stay.
